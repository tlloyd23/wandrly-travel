Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Wandrly. By using this site, you agree to the points below. We've tried to keep this short and plain, because nobody enjoys reading legal pages on a travel site.

Wandrly is an independent, early-stage project. Trip plans are generated using a mix of curated notes and AI, and should always be treated as a starting point, not a booking confirmation or professional travel advice.

1. What Wandrly is

Wandrly helps you plan trips by suggesting destinations, routes, activities and rough budgets. Some of this content comes from a hand-built collection of notes and recommendations, and some is generated by AI based on your search. We do our best to keep both accurate, but travel information changes constantly (opening hours, prices, visa rules, safety conditions) and we can't guarantee everything will be correct or current.

2. Always double-check before you book

Before booking flights, accommodation, tours or anything else based on a Wandrly itinerary, please verify the details independently, especially prices, opening times, entry requirements and safety advisories. Wandrly is a planning aid, not a travel agency, and we are not liable for any loss, cost or inconvenience arising from relying on information provided here.

3. Your data

Wandrly stores some information locally in your browser (such as your search history and daily usage count) to make the site work better and to manage fair use of AI features. This data stays on your device and is not sold to third parties. If Wandrly later introduces accounts, a separate privacy policy will explain what account data is collected and how it's used.

4. Affiliate links

Wandrly may include links to third-party booking services (such as accommodation, flights or activity providers). If you book through these links, Wandrly may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects which destinations or activities are recommended, and recommendations are based on genuine usefulness, not commission rates.

5. Acceptable use

Please don't use Wandrly to attempt to overload, scrape at scale, or abuse the AI search features (for example, automating large volumes of requests). Fair use limits exist to keep the site free and usable for everyone.
